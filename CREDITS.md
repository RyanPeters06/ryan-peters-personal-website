# Credits — bundled third-party assets

Everything here is bundled into the build (no runtime CDN fetches). All
assets are usable without attribution, but they're credited anyway.

## 3D models

- **Player avatar** — `src/assets/rogue.glb`
  From **KayKit : Adventurers Character Pack (1.0)** by
  **Kay Lousberg** (https://kaylousberg.com).
  License: **CC0 1.0 Universal** (public domain). The "Rogue" character
  is used with its adventurer weapons hidden; animations (Idle, Walking,
  Cheer) are driven at runtime.
